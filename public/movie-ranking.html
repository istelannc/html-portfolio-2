The Best Movies According to Angela

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

A Walk To Remember

A love story so sweet, sincere and positive that it sneaks past the defenses built up in this age of irony.

Serendipity

The heartwarming romantic comedy Serendipity is a fun and enchanting tale.

Rookie

Rookie is a lighthearted romance that normalizes same-sex relationships and treats it with as much kilig as most mainstream movies.
